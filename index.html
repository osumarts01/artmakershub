Digital artists usually need tools like drawing tablets, graphic design software, and creative apps that help them sketch, paint, and edit artwork digitally.

Beginners often start with affordable drawing tablets that are easy to use and compatible with popular design software. Choosing the right tablet depends on budget, features, and comfort.

Yes. Many artists sell digital artwork through online marketplaces, print-on-demand platforms, and personal websites. With the right tools and marketing strategies, artists can build a successful online presence.
